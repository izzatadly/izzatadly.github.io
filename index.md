## Research
My research explores how information frictions and prior beliefs influence household financial decisions.

### Working Papers
* **The Role of Prior Beliefs in Housing Expectations** (2026)  
    *Abstract:* This paper investigates how heterogeneous prior beliefs about market trends dictate the formation of housing expectations.
    
* **Inattentive Refinancing Decisions** (Job Market Paper, 2026)  
    *Abstract:* I develop a structural model to identify parameters governing the rational inattention of households when making mortgage refinancing decisions.

### Parameter Identification Model
In my structural work, I define the transition from utility primitives to empirical regressors. For example, given a utility function $U(c, h)$, the first-order condition (FOC) identifies the marginal rate of substitution:
$$\frac{\partial U / \partial h}{\partial U / \partial c} = p_h$$
where $h$ is housing services and $p_h$ is the relative price of housing.
